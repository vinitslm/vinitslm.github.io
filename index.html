Who I Am

About Me

With over two decades of expertise in Corporate and Financial Education, I have encompassed roles spanning Research, Teaching, Training, and Consulting across various facets of Finance and Economics.

My professional journey has seen me collaborate within multicultural environments and multidisciplinary teams, granting me a comprehensive understanding of both Corporate Laws and Social Sciences. Currently serving as Associate Director & Head of Training and Placement at MRIIRS, Faridabad, I bridge the gap between academia and industry.

A Fellow Member of the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI), I have presented research at premier institutions including IIM Ahmedabad and IIT-ISM Dhanbad, with publications in Scopus-indexed and ABDC-ranked journals.
